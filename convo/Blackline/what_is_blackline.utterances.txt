BlackLine is a tool that helps automate the account reconciliation process. It is capable of integration with ERP to auto interface GL and sub ledger balances for reconciliation from ERP system to Blackline.

It also has an auto certification in place where in the tool will certify a reconcilation based on enabled logics without any manual interventions. It is also capable of importing line items from ERP to the respective reconciliations automatically using the Transaction matching module feature.

Blackline support multiple languages and currencies and currecy rates can be uploaded to convert all amounts to a standard reporting currency automatically. The tool offers good controls over your process - automatic segragation of duties and audit trail amongst other benefits
